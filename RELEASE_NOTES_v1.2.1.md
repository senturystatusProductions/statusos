# StatusOS v1.2.1 – Premium UI + Flexible Habit Goals

## Added
- Premium dark-first visual system with deeper panels, restrained gold accents, and reduced white surfaces.
- Daily, weekly, and monthly habit schedules.
- Custom targets such as Workout 3 times per week.
- Period-based streaks that reward meeting commitments instead of demanding daily activity.
- Progress bars and current-period completion counts for every habit.
- Backward-compatible migration of existing daily habits.
