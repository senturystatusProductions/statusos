STATUSOS v0.3.1 INSTALL

1. Keep a backup of your current working folder.
2. Copy the contents of this ZIP into the ROOT of your StatusOS repository.
3. Confirm index.html, app.js, style.css and service-worker.js are directly in the repository root.
4. Commit and push.
5. Open the site and press Ctrl + Shift + R.
6. On iPhone, close and reopen the installed app. If needed, open the site once in Safari.
