# StatusOS v3.2.0 — Finance OS

- Unified finance dashboard for income, expenses, profit and outstanding balances
- Expense tracking by business category
- Invoice tracking with draft, sent, partial, paid and overdue states
- Client lifetime value and outstanding project balances
- Six-month cash-flow view and revenue by service
- CSV export for bookkeeping
- Existing project payments and revenue records are reused automatically
- No database migration required
